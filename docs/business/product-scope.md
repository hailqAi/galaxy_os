# Product scope

Galaxy OS is the internal project-centered ERP for Galaxy Centre. `Project` will connect design, product selection, costing, quotation, contract, procurement, production, logistics, receivables, payables, installation, handover, warranty, after-sales, internal approvals, and marketing.

Sprint 0 implements infrastructure only. Approved delivery order is maintained in [the roadmap](../roadmap.md); detailed lifecycle steps are in [workflows](workflows.md).
