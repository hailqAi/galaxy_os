# Business workflows

The future lifecycle runs from lead and customer discovery through design, selection, estimate, quotation, contract, purchasing, production, transport, financial settlement, installation, inspection, handover, warranty, support, internal approvals, and marketing.

Sprint 1 establishes the staff access workflow: an administrator creates a user profile and organization membership, assigns departments and current roles, and may later disable access without deleting history. Role, organization, department, and user changes are audited. The last active system administrator remains protected.

ERP and project operations take priority. Each later sprint must define approved fields, permissions, transitions, versioning, and audit events before implementation.
