# Backlog

The ordered product backlog is the [roadmap](roadmap.md). Before each sprint, convert only that sprint into approved behavior, data, security, migration, and acceptance criteria in `PLANS.md`.

Unscheduled technical work must have evidence, impact, and an owner. Speculative infrastructure and generic abstractions are not backlog items.

Production identity-provider selection, login, password/token flows, and SSO remain outside Sprint 1 until separately approved. Sprint 2 must reuse the Sprint 1 `CurrentActor` authorization boundary.
